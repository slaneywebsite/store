About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free delivery in Wexford and the South East?

Yes, we provide free delivery across Wexford and the South East of Ireland on all furniture orders. Your items will be delivered directly to your home by our trusted delivery team.

How long does delivery take?

Most in-stock items are delivered within 14 working days. If an item requires special ordering, our team will confirm the estimated delivery time at checkout.

Can I see the furniture in person?

Yes! You can visit our Slaneyside Furniture showroom in Enniscorthy, Co. Wexford, where you can view our full range of sofas, bedroom furniture, dining sets, and more.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes, our delivery team can assemble your furniture on request. Assembly options are available for beds, wardrobes, and larger items.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and Klarna. With Klarna's Buy Now, Pay Later option, you can spread the cost of your furniture over flexible instalments.

Do you have clearance or sale items?

Yes! Check our Clearance section for great offers on sofas, dining sets, and more at discounted prices.

Do you deliver outside of Ireland?

At the moment, we only deliver within Ireland. If you're outside Ireland, please contact us to discuss special arrangements.

Can I order custom furniture sizes or finishes?

Some of our furniture collections can be customised (fabric, colour, or finish). Contact our team for details.
